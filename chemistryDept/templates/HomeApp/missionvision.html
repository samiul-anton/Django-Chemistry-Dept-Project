Vision and Mission

Vision Statement

We build excellent academic programs with national and international reputation in chemistry, chemical engineering or biomedical engineering, for educating students with diverse backgrounds as next-generation work force and community leaders to improve human life by solving contemporary challenges

Mission Statement

To reach our goal of building the excellent academic programs with national and international reputation, we are committed to the following missions

We educate students with an integrative approach of combining the knowledge and skills in theories, computational methods, and experiments into the curriculum design for identifying, analyzing, and solving problems.

We stimulate and inspire students to contribute innovative solutions to contemporary challenges, such as those in sustainability and energy, biomedical fields, and artificial intelligence, through research, internship, community service, or other high-impact activities.

We are devoted to advance the professional and career development of students and faculty, by promoting excellent performances in education, research, and service.
